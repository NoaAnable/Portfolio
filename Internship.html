From August to October 2024, I undertook a placement with Ten Hammers interactive training where I was tasked with asset development for aviation VR training environments.

During my placement, I created assets such as cabin doors, a plane galley, an engine rack, and an oxygen tank. Through these tasks, I was responsible for the modeling, UVs, texturing, and baking lighting. I was also responsible for making this asset appropriate for VR use with optimised modeling and textures
