A micro world, designed around harsh weather which is randomly simulated. This world accommodates small inhabitants to hide from bad weather.

The idea springs from the contempt of microbiomes, narrating the story of a smaller civilization located within a dystopian world. Harsh weather was achieved through particle systems, lighting and sonic shifts within the environment. The inhabitants interact are built through behaviour trees, within a world built on stylized assets.
