Noelia Martinez is a 3D generalist and environmental artist based in Melbourne, Australia. She is currently studying a Bachelors of Design (Digital Media) at RMIT University. Noelia has an interest in building worlds and stories through prop and environmental modelling, telling a story through environment. At RMIT, Noelia has been able to build and explore her skills in 3D and game environments, whilst also exploring other digital medium forms, softwares, and technologies.


Throughout her studies Noelia has taken part in many group assignments, including creating and running an exhibition. where she was responsible for creating a Unity environment with others, managing the exhibition space, and archiving of the event. Her latest work explores a living fish tank environment in Unity exploring an isolated dystopian world with its own environmental driven atmosphere and characters. She is currently planning on creating a small demo environment/game to further grow and refine her skills.
